UX flow chart

Scenario 1

To submit a film, the user has to log in / sign up for a filmfreeway account. They then have to click on “My projects” in the menu at the top of the page where they can then add a project by clicking on the button. They will then have to start the process of filling in the submission form and saving it when done. They will have then submitted a film in their account. All of these flow charts are created in InDesign to test the way they looked. The final Adobe XD design is at the end of this document.

Scenario 2

To find a festival that accepts music videos the user has to log into their account and click on “browse festivals” in the menu. Then , the user has to go through the filter to filter out the festivals that do not accept music videos by scrolling down to the “categories” section. They then have to click on “music video” to filter out all of the other options. The user then has to click on one of festival options and click on the “submit now” button to add a music video project. When they do this they will have to finish the project submission form and save the project. which they can edit later or immediately submit.
